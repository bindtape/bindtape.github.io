BINDTAPE
Music Social Network
Bindtape is an app for sharing the entire musical experience by merging images, text and playlists.
Now you can instantly share your music and art experience with a maximum number of friends.


Bindtape is integrated with Apple Music
and you need Apple Music subscription to use it.

Release in June 2018.
Bindtape lets users share the mood of the moment quickly and easily. It connects to the music streaming services the user is subscribed to.

Currently, Bindtape is integrated with IOS and Apple Music.

Bindtape posts include images and captions that enrich the music sharing. Bindtape friend feed is a stream of synesthetic experiences.
